The Key Elements of Good Photo

With the improvement of mobile phone penetration. We experience a lot of photos on the internet. Today, everyone can shoot a photo, but what will let your photo stand out? What will make you a good photographer? There are three basic principles of a good photo:

  1. A good photo should have a distinct subject, that the subject is very clear and unambiguous to the viewer. It can a person, a group of people, a thing, a group of things, or a story.
  2. The viewer’s attention should be directed to the subject of the photo. The viewer should able to identify the subject at first glance.
  3. The frame of the photo should be concise. That anything will distract viewer’s attention toward the subject should be eliminated.

When you press your shutter, you should always remind you three questions:


  1. What subject am I presenting?
  2. How could I attract viewer’s attention toward the subject?
  3. Is there anything will distract viewer’s attention toward the subject?
